About

The person behind KnowYourSolar.

Vihari Tejomurtula

Creator of KnowYourSolar

I'm a computer engineering student at UC Santa Cruz with a focus on practical applications of machine learning and web development.

I built KnowYourSolar because I saw a gap in the market: homeowners considering solar face a confusing landscape of sales-driven installers with financial incentives to recommend systems regardless of actual feasibility. Online calculators provide rough estimates but lack the nuance to account for shading, roof conditions, or local cost variations.

By separating assessment from installation, KnowYourSolar provides neutral analysis. When the evidence suggests solar isn't viable for a property, that's what the report will say.

This project is maintained as an independent effort. I do not have partnerships with installers, utilities, or equipment manufacturers.
