Privacy & Data Handling

What we collect, why we collect it, and how we handle your information.

What we collect

To generate a solar feasibility report, we collect:

  • Property address: Street address used to determine roof orientation, local climate data, and applicable utility rates
  • Roof photographs: Images of your roof submitted for analysis of pitch, orientation, shading, and usable area
  • Contact information: Email address to deliver the completed report

We do not collect financial information, social security numbers, or detailed personal data beyond what is necessary for the assessment.

Why we collect it

Each piece of information serves a specific analytical purpose:

  • Address: Required to access location-specific solar irradiance data, determine roof orientation from satellite imagery, and apply correct utility rate structures
  • Photographs: Enable detailed assessment of roof characteristics that affect solar production, including pitch angle, shading from nearby trees or structures, and available installation area
  • Email: Needed only to deliver your completed report and any follow-up clarifications

How long we retain it

We retain data according to these timelines:

  • Roof photographs: Deleted within 30 days after report delivery unless you explicitly request longer retention for comparison with future assessments
  • Property address and report outputs: Retained for up to 2 years to support methodology improvements and quality assurance
  • Aggregated, anonymized data: May be retained indefinitely for statistical analysis and methodology refinement

You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing info@knowyoursolar.com. We will confirm deletion within 14 days.

Whether we share it

We do not share your property-specific information with solar installers, lead generation services, or marketing companies.

We may share data in these limited circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent: If you request that we share your report with a specific installer or other party
  • Aggregated and anonymized: Statistical data that cannot identify individual properties may be used in research or methodology documentation
  • Legal requirements: If required by law or valid legal process

How we protect it

We implement standard security practices:

  • Data encrypted in transit using TLS
  • Access limited to personnel directly involved in report generation
  • Regular deletion of data past retention periods
  • No storage of data on personal devices or unsecured systems

Third-party services

We use the following third-party services in our analysis:

  • NREL PVWatts: Address submitted to retrieve solar irradiance data; NREL does not receive identifying information beyond location
  • Google Maps API: Address geocoded to determine precise coordinates; subject to Google's privacy policy
  • OpenAI API: Roof photographs analyzed for pitch and shading; images not stored by OpenAI per API terms

We do not control these third parties' data practices. You should review their privacy policies if you have concerns about their data handling.

Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the data we hold about your property
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing where consent is the legal basis

To exercise these rights, contact us at info@knowyoursolar.com.

Changes to this policy

We will update this page if our data practices change. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the bottom of this page.

This policy does not create contractual obligations. It describes our current practices as of the date below.
