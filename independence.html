Independence Charter

Our commitments to remain independent and aligned with homeowner clarity.

Why this exists

Solar decisions are hard—not because the technology is complicated, but because incentives are often misaligned.

KnowYourSolar exists to answer one question honestly:

Does solar make sense for this home>?

This charter explains how we protect that answer from influence.

What we will never do

These are more than promises. These are constraints.

  • We will not accept commissions, referral fees, or per-installation payments from installers.
  • We will not change results based on who requests a report or who may benefit from it.
  • We will never recommend solar unless the numbers justify it.
  • We will not suppress or omit findings that suggest solar is not viable for a home.
  • We will not use language that overstates benefits, suggests certainity, or creates urgency.

Transparency Clause

Every report is accompanied by clear disclosures, including:

  • All major assumptions used in production and savings calculations
  • Known limitations and sources of uncertainty
  • The geographic scope and callibration of our analysis
  • The methodology version and date used to generate the report

Nothing material is hidden behind sales language or fine print.

Conflict disclosure standard

KnowYourSolar is built so our recommendations do not depend on who benefits from them.

If a relationship ever introduces a potential conflict, it will be stated plainly alongside our reports, with a clear explanation of how report integrity is preserved.

Disclosure is not a fallback. It is part of how independence is enforced.

How this charter is enforced

This charter is not aspirational. It describes how we actually operate.

If we cannot maintain these standards, we will update this page to reflect reality—rather than allow our principles and practices to drift apart.

We believe clarity is more valuable than appearances.

What this means for you

You can read a KnowYourSolar report knowing that:

  • No one is paid more if you install solar
  • No one is penalized if you don't
  • The recommendation exists to inform your decision, not to move you toward a transaction

That is the whole point
