How we stay independent

We do not sell solar systems. We do not receive commissions from installers. We do not adjust outputs based on who requests the report.

Our analysis may conclude that solar is not financially viable for your property. When that happens, we explain why and suggest alternatives worth considering.

When we say "not recommended"

We may recommend against solar installation when:

  • Heavy shading or poor roof orientation
  • Upcoming roof replacement
  • Limited space for a viable system
  • Low usage or low utility rates
  • Permitting or HOA restrictions

How estimates are produced

Roof Photo Analysis

We analyze roof photos to extract pitch and shading factors. This provides accurate data on your roof's orientation and any obstacles that might reduce solar production.

Location & Climate Data

We combine this with your address to determine orientation and local climate data from NREL PVWatts. This ensures production estimates reflect your actual location's sunlight patterns.

System Sizing

Standard system sizing assumptions are applied based on available roof area and estimated usage. We determine a realistic system size that fits your property constraints.

Production Modeling

Production estimates use historical weather data for your location. This provides realistic annual output expectations based on actual climate patterns, not best-case scenarios.

Cost & Savings

Cost assumptions are based on recent regional installation averages. Savings calculations account for utility rate structures and net metering policies in your area.

Currently Calibrated

Currently calibrated for Santa Cruz, CA. All cost data, utility rates, and regional assumptions are specific to this area.

Limitations and uncertainty

Our estimates are based on assumptions that may not match your exact situation. Actual results can differ due to:

  • Changes in utility rates or net metering policies
  • Variations in weather patterns from historical averages
  • Differences between estimated and actual electricity usage
  • Installation costs varying by contractor and specific roof complexity
  • Equipment degradation rates differing from manufacturer specifications

This report provides a feasibility assessment, not a guarantee of outcomes.

Even if solar isn't right for you

When solar doesn't make financial sense, other energy actions may still be worth looking into. We do not provide these services, but you may want to consider:

  • Home energy audit to identify efficiency improvements
  • Insulation upgrades that reduce heating and cooling costs
  • LED lighting conversion and smart thermostat installation
  • Electric vehicle charging rate optimization if applicable
  • Community solar programs that don't require roof installation
